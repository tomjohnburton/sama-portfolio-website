I'm Sama Mirshahvalad. Iranian born artist, now based in London specialised in Contemporary Sculpture and Three-Dimensional Art.

After spending more than 12 years studying and creating in the most prestigious art institutions and galleries across Europe, I decided to focus on what I love most: the dialogue between form, space, and human experience.

Now I'm opening my own Sculpture Studio.
